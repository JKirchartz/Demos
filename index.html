The Goods

  1. HTML5 notepad app

    Auto-saves every 500 ms. & on close, in 5 lines of javascript, HTML5 rocks! (ok, I added a polyfill, to support older browsers, so it's a lil' more than that.)

  2. css3 multi-button

    Hiding extra content and links in buttons.

  3. bookmarklets

    A Collection

  4. ds-10 buttons

    Emulating the drum-pad button animation from the Korg DS-10

  5. HSL fun

    HSL color spinner.

  6. Push Your Luck

    A clone of the popular gameshow, no whammies yet, just the animation & click detection. It's a little unreliable on restarting the game & everybody's a winner! It's designed for full-screen mode so for most browsers hit f11 and give it a go.

  7. Sewn

    that new "stitched" trend with a png texture and border:dashed.
